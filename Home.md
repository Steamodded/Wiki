### If you have no experience with coding, here are some useful tips to get started
- [Your First Mod](https://github.com/Steamodded/smods/wiki/Your-First-Mod)
- take a browse through [The Lua Refrence Manual](https://www.lua.org/manual/5.1/) or [Programing in Lua](https://www.lua.org/pil/contents.html).
- Taking a look through [An overall example mod](https://github.com/Dragokillfist/Example-Balatro-Mod) or the [SMODS example](https://github.com/Steamodded/examples) which is focused on more specific functions and is made by the SMODS team.
